PROTOTYPE USER INSTRUCTIONS

Welcome to the interactive mock simulation. This physical model utilizes advanced neural intelligence to split items dynamically. Experience it with these simple steps:

Drag & Drop Shelf: Grab any organic (apple, onion, banana) or dry waste (bottle, can, box) item from the left tray and **drop it inside the 3D scene**.

Interact or Click: If you're on a mobile device or prefer click-mode, you can **click/tap** any item in the tray to automatically process it.

Voice Triggers: Activate voice mode, and say "wet", "onion", "can" or "dry" to instruct sorting instantly.
